Welcome to Distance Detectors

This website serves as an introduction to our project. Our project involved using a Raspberry Pi with a VL53L0X Time-of-Flight (ToF) sensor to measure distances. The objective was to design and implement a device that emits sound alerts when objects or individuals approach a wall or another obstacle. The device functions as an auditory backup camera, providing real-time feedback to prevent collisions. Navigate through the menu to explore more.
